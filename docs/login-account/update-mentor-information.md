        <h2>Update Mentor Information</h2>  
        <p>
            When a mentor logs in for the first time, they must provide necessary details to complete their profile. 
            This ensures accurate mentor information, improves matching with mentees, and enhances the overall platform experience.
        </p>
        <h2>Use Case Scenario</h2> 
        <table border="1">
            <tr>
                <th>Use Case</th>
                <th>First-Time Mentor Login and Profile Completion</th>
            </tr>
            <tr>
                <th>Actor(s)</th>
                <td>Mentor</td>
            </tr>
            <tr>
                <th>Goal</th>
                <td>Complete the mentor profile with required information before accessing the platform.</td>
            </tr>
            <tr>
                <th>Preconditions</th>
                <td>The mentor has successfully given an account credentials and is logging in for the first time.</td>
            </tr>
            <tr>
                <th>Main Scenario</th>
                <td>
                    1. Mentor logs into the platform for the first time.<br>
                    2. System detects first-time login and redirects to the profile update page.<br>
                    3. Mentor is required to fill out necessary details such as:<br>
                      - Year level<br>
                      - Expertise and specialization<br>
                      - Availability schedule<br>
                      - Social links<br>
                      - Optional experiences<br>
                    4. Mentor submits the form.<br>
                    5. System validates the information and saves the profile.<br>
                    6. Mentor gains full access to the platform and can start engaging with mentees.<br>
                </td>
            </tr>
            <tr>
                <th>Outcome</th>
                <td>The mentor successfully updates their profile and can now use the platform's features.</td>
            </tr>
