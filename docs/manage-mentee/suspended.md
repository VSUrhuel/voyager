    <h2>Suspended Mentees</h2>  
    <p>
        The Suspended Mentees section displays a list of mentees whose accounts have been suspended by a mentor. 
        Mentors can review the suspension and have the option to unsuspend a mentee, allowing them to regain access to mentorship services.
    </p>
    <h2>Use Case Scenario</h2> 
    <table border="1">
        <tr>
            <th>Use Case</th>
            <th>Managing Suspended Mentees</th>
        </tr>
        <tr>
            <th>Actor(s)</th>
            <td>Mentor</td>
        </tr>
        <tr>
            <th>Goal</th>
            <td>Review and manage suspended mentees, including the option to unsuspend them.</td>
        </tr>
        <tr>
            <th>Preconditions</th>
            <td>The mentor is logged into the app and has at least one suspended mentee.</td>
        </tr>
        <tr>
            <th>Main Scenario</th>
            <td>
                1. Mentor logs into the platform.<br>
                2. Mentor navigates to the "Suspended Mentees" section.<br>
                3. Mentor reviews the list of suspended mentees.<br>
                4. Mentor can take the following actions:<br>
                   &nbsp;   &nbsp;- Unsuspend a mentee if they meet the necessary conditions.<br>
                   &nbsp;   &nbsp;- Keep the suspension if the mentee still violates guidelines.<br>
                5. System updates the mentee status accordingly.<br>
                6. Mentee receives a notification about their updated status.<br>
            </td>
        </tr>
        <tr>
            <th>Outcome</th>
            <td>The mentor successfully manages suspended mentees, either reinstating or keeping them suspended.</td>
        </tr>
    </table>
